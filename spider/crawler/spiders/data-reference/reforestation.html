Acacia holosericea A.Cunn. ex G.Don Main specimen
Fabaceae
{ Reforestation }

  • Planting advice and requirements :

    Acacia holosericea is a fast growing species and it is able to flourish in a wide range of soils. It thrives in open areas.
    The species is propagated by seeds.

  • Seed treatments :

    24 hours of soaking in water accelerates the germination process.
